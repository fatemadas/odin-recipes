Hobi's Fruit Croffle

Description

A fun and delicious breakfast dish made by Jung Hosoek aka Hobi

Ingredients

  • 2 premade croissant dough
  • Granulated sugar

  • Toppings:

    • Apricot Goat Cheese
    • Blueberry or wild berry Goat Cheese
    • Vanilla Ice-cream
    • Maple Syrup
    • Fresh fruits like bananas and tangerines

Steps

  1. Preheat the waffle maker to medium heat
  2. Roll up the croissant dough into crescent shapes
  3. Roll the crescents in sugar and cook it in the waffle maker until they are cooked through, keeping an eye that the sugar doesn't burn.
  4. To serve: On a plate, place one of the croffles top with a few slices of apricot goat cheese, and add the other croffle on top. Add a small slice of the blueberry/wild berry goat cheese.
  5. Add a scoop of ice cream. On the side, place your cut fruits and drizzle them with some maple syrup and enjoy!!
