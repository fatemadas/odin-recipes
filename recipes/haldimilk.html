Haldi Doodh

Description

Haldi Doodh is a warm comforting beverage made with Turmeric and Milk. It soothes the throat and has general anti inflammatory properties

Ingredients

  • Turmeric
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Take 250ml of milk in a Mug
  2. Microwave it for 1 minute
  3. Add 1 tsp of turmeric powder and mix
  4. Serve warm and enjoy!
